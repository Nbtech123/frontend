About NB Tech Solutions

Established in October 2024, we help customers build secure, stable, and well‑organized IT infrastructure. Our approach is simple: assess requirements, recommend what fits your site and budget, and deliver a clean installation with clear handover.

What we do

We deliver end‑to‑end deployments across surveillance, structured cabling, networking, firewalls, Wi‑Fi access points, peripherals/software, and VoIP/video conferencing.

How we work

We prioritize neat cabling, stable configurations, and documentation-friendly setups. You’ll get a clear plan, transparent next steps, and professional execution.

Who we serve

Homes, offices, retail stores, warehouses, and multi‑site businesses—anywhere reliable connectivity and security systems are critical.
